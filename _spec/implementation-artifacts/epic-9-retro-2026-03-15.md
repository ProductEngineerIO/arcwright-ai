## Key Insights

1. For SCM behavior, passing tests are not enough unless assertions are **semantically exact** to AC wording.
2. Real-git integration tests are high value when scoped to critical chain behavior, especially around conflict/failure paths.
3. Sprint/state hygiene should stay synchronized with technical completion and review outcomes, not deferred to cleanup passes.
4. Explicitly recording review follow-up closure in story artifacts reduces ambiguity at epic closeout.
